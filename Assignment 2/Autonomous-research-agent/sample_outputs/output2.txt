Title: Blockchain in Finance

INTRODUCTION
Blockchain is a decentralized digital ledger technology that is transforming the financial sector by enhancing transparency, security, and efficiency in transactions.

KEY FINDINGS
- Enables secure and transparent transactions
- Eliminates intermediaries in financial systems
- Reduces fraud and corruption
- Improves cross-border payments
- Supports smart contracts

CHALLENGES
- Scalability issues
- High energy consumption
- Regulatory uncertainty
- Lack of awareness and adoption
- Integration with existing systems

FUTURE SCOPE
- Decentralized finance (DeFi)
- Smart contract automation
- Global payment systems
- Tokenization of assets
- Digital currencies (CBDCs)

CONCLUSION
Blockchain has the potential to revolutionize finance by making systems more secure and transparent, but it requires regulatory clarity and technological advancements for mass adoption.
