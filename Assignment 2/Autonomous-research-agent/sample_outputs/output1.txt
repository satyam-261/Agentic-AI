Title: Impact of AI in Healthcare

INTRODUCTION
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is transforming healthcare by enabling faster diagnosis, improving treatment accuracy, and automating administrative processes. It plays a major role in predictive analytics, medical imaging, and personalized medicine.

KEY FINDINGS
- AI improves diagnostic accuracy using machine learning models
- Reduces workload on healthcare professionals
- Enables early disease detection
- Enhances patient care through personalized treatment
- Automates hospital operations and record management

CHALLENGES
- Data privacy and security concerns
- High implementation cost
- Lack of skilled professionals
- Ethical concerns in decision-making
- Regulatory limitations

FUTURE SCOPE
- AI-powered robotic surgeries
- Real-time health monitoring systems
- Predictive healthcare models
- Fully automated hospitals
- Integration with wearable devices

CONCLUSION
AI is revolutionizing healthcare by improving efficiency and outcomes. However, challenges like cost, ethics, and data security must be addressed for widespread adoption.
